## Funnel by Device Results

Desktop
- View → Cart: 20.32%
- Cart → Checkout: 76.72%
- Checkout → Purchase: 44.85%

Mobile
- View → Cart: 20.72%
- Cart → Checkout: 77.17%
- Checkout → Purchase: 46.65%

Tablet
- View → Cart: 19.13%
- Cart → Checkout: 79.35%
- Checkout → Purchase: 44.29%

- ## Key Insight

- Desktop has the largest user volume and therefore the highest revenue impact potential.
- Mobile conversion is slightly higher in checkout → purchase.
- Tablet volume is small and lower priority for optimization.
